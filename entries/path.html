Overview

PATH provides 24/7 rail service between Manhattan and New Jersey. It is commonly used for daily commuting and airport access via connections.

Lines

Core routes include 33 St–Journal Square, 33 St–Hoboken, World Trade Center–Journal Square, and World Trade Center–Newark.

Key stations

Major stations include World Trade Center, 33 St, Exchange Place, Journal Square, Hoboken, and Newark Penn.
