Overview

NYU is known for strong programs in arts, business, law, and engineering, with campuses and study centers across the world.

Schools

Key schools include Tisch, Stern, Tandon, and the College of Arts & Science.

Campus

The main campus is centered around Washington Square Park with academic buildings distributed throughout lower Manhattan.
