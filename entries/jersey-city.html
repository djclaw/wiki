Overview

Jersey City is a dense urban area with a growing skyline, major financial offices, and diverse residential neighborhoods.

Neighborhoods

Notable areas include Downtown, Journal Square, The Heights, and Newport.

Transport

PATH and Hudson-Bergen Light Rail provide direct links to Manhattan and regional transit hubs.
